Structure of the Puzzle Hunt

This hunt will be split into 2 parts, part 1 on the first day and part 2 on the second day. After you have solved everything (or voided everything), you will need to use all the answers on that day to solve the metapuzzle on that day. The metapuzzle on each day will lead you to 2 important clues to solve the murder.

  • On day 1, the metapuzzle leads to the murder location.
  • On day 2, the metapuzzle leads to the murder weapon.

Once you are done with both days, on the third day the final puzzle will make use of 6 of the answers (3 in day 1 and 3 in day 2) as an answer. With that, you will be able to solve the murder mystery by fingding out the murder!

Submissions
After logging in, you will able to submit the answer for each puzzle on the respective puzzle pages. Please contact your OGL for the Team Name and Password. Some important points to note:
  • Only one person from each team needs to submit the answer for each puzzle.
  • Submit your answer using only the characters a-zA-Z, and spaces.
  • After each submission, you should receive one of 2 responses. ("Correct" or "Incorrect")
  • You can go to the puzzle list for an overview of your team's progress.
Hints
Each team will be given 10 hint credits at the start of the hunt. Hints for all puzzles cost 1 credit, including metapuzzles and the final puzzle. You can be assured that if more than 1 team requests a hint for the same puzzle, they will receive the same hint.
Voiding
If you realise the question is too hard, you can choose to void it without deducting your puzzles. With that you can get the answer to the question so that you can use it for the metapuzzles/final puzzle. Void at your own risk, any voids will not be reversible.
Scoring
We use a simple scoring system for this puzzle hunt. The leaderboard can be found here (Leaderboard as of now is still not ready yet!!) Details are as follows:
  • Each team starts with 10 base points.
  • Each puzzle is worth 1-10 points, depending on the difficulty.
  • Each metapuzzle is worth 6 points.
  • The final puzzle (metametapuzzle) is worth 10 points.
