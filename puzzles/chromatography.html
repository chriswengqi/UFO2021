Chromatography
Many NUS researches have been successful and widely known. You have recently heard of an unknown solution in the School of Computing for a few decades and scientists are still unable to figure out what it is. So you decide to travel back time and look into it. You ask your friend Brenda (who is a chemist) to help you check out what it is. However, you and Brenda get into a fight just before the results come out and she decides to unfriend you. She does not go easy on you and sends you a weird message before letting you view the result. Can you figure out what the message is?
