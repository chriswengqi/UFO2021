Finding Karen's Son
Your task is to help Ms Karen find her long-lost son, Jiin Jia. With your superb detective skills, you manage to get hold of five different passports that might be Jiin Jia’s fake passports over the past 5 years. Perhaps by tracing her son’s travel log you can find out what her son’s favourite brand is?
