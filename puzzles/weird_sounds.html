Weird Sounds
Your 1st time travel journey takes you to the Jurassic Era, where you see dinosaurs all around you. You stumble upon a type of creature that is not recorded in the history books before. Fascinated, you inch towards it slowly, only for it to make a very weird screech. What does the screech sound like?
