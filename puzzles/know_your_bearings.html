Know Your Bearings
You have travelled to year 5028 where aliens are slowly invading and conquering the earth. The aliens have developed a booster that causes the earth to rotate backwards! You, as an experienced detective, are the only hope to save the world! You found a wrecked paper along with a side note and a few strings that was dropped right next to the booster. Hurry and figure out what the passcode is to stop the booster before the world ends!
