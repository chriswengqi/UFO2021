You have an opportunity to visit Masterchef Singapore season 10th winner, Gordon Hamlay, who is known as a chiffon cake king. He wants you to solve a word for him before letting you read his secret recipe (Who knows! You can be a billionaire with his recipe!!). He showed you a recipe that he is currently working on and he whispered “remember to always use your right hand for dry ingredients”. Can you figure out what the word is?
Ingredients for chocolate chiffon cake Weight (by %)
Egg Yolk 5
Egg White 12
Oil 8
Milk 9
Cake Flour 13
Cocoa Powder 13
Corn Flour 8
Cream of Tartar 4
Baking Powder 8
Baking Soda 4
Sugar 8
Salt 4

RECIPE

  1. Whisk the egg yolk in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add baking powder followed by baking soda into the mixing bowl.
  3. Sift the cake flour first and then the cocoa powder into it.
  4. Add egg whites into a mixer bowl and whisk until soft peak, then add sugar into it and continue to whisk until stiff peak.
  5. Prepare the oil in a separate bowl and add in salt.
  6. Mix gently and add in milk.
  7. Add corn flour into it, mix gently then lastly add in cream of tartar
