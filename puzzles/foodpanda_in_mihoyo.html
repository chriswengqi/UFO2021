Foodpanda in Mihoyo
You land in a time where the Mihoyo kingdom reigns over the world. You realised that you are short in cash, and decided to work part time as a delivery man at Wanmin Restaurant to earn some money to continue your quest of making an impact in the detective world. Below are the orders that you have to complete before you call it a day. Before leaving, your boss reminds you to take note of the stars that your customers give you for your dish. Where is your final destination?
  • Deliver Chicken-Mushroom Skewer to a girl wearing a deep violet overcoat. Inside she wears a pale lavender top with a high violet collar and ruffled dark blue skirt. She is known to enjoy her food under a sunny moment
  • Deliver Crab Roe Tofu to a girl wearing a black leotard underneath a red, sleeveless, qipao dress. She stays in the north and owns a dipper.
  • Deliver Crab, Ham and Vege Bake to a young woman wearing a red and black xiezhi hat, and a red and black cropped top with detached white sleeves and a white collar. She likes to play with scarlet smoke.
  • Deliver Crispy Potato Shrimp Platter to a girl wearing a traditional red shirt with a mandarin collar, a black coat and black shorts with gold accents. She can be found lingering around a walnut tree
  • Deliver Grilled Tiger Fish to a woman dressed like a nun. Her outfit consists of a long white and black coif with red lining, a white sleeveless turtleneck top, long white gloves and a long black and red slit skirt.
  • Deliver Lotus Seed and Bird Egg Soup to a woman wearing a purple and white dress with open slits at the sides and gold embroidery, a large witch's hat, black lace stockings, and black high heels. She looks eerily similar to Phoebe in Friends.
  • Deliver Radish Veggie Soup to a guy wearing a dark brown coat that is thick and adorned with gold trimmings and tassels, ending with black fur-trims. Inside he wears a black dress shirt, a long white vest, and a black ascot with a red gem. He makes deals with people while looking them in the eye.
  • Deliver Moon Pie and Northern Smoked Chicken to a guy wearing a grey jacket with dark red and light grey details, grey pants, and black boots and gloves. He likes to leave his jacket unbuttoned and acts like a child when he does not get what he wants. Do use his real name when you address him.
  • Deliver Golden Crabs to a girl wearing a jumper-like outfit composed of dark brown leather shorts, a red hoodie-like top, red stockings, and white thigh-high boots embellished with gold linings and symbols. #FFBF00
