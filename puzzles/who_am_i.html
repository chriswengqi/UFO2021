WHO AM I
You try to replicate the sound in the previous puzzle and activate a wormhole that brings you to America before Columbus. The indigenous people at that time have predicted that a scandalous figure will plague your life during your generation. They give you the following riddle to solve.

Part I

Arnold Schwarzenegger has a long one. Michael J. Fox has a short one. Madonna does not use hers. Bill Clinton always uses his. The Pope never uses his. What is it?

Part II

With Part I in mind, scramble the letters
A,K,R,Z,A

Part III

Inserting the answer behind, what do you get?
_ a _ _ b _ _ _ _ _

Who is this scandalous figure?
