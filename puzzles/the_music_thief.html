The Music Thief
Apparently you realised that you are not the only one who can time travel, there are others who are abusing the time travel powers given to them for misdeed as well! This time, a company called Bloomsbury asks you to chase after a thief who travelled to the past to steal their publishing rights for their magical books (actually quite well-known among people in this generation). Even worse, the book thief even tampers in some of the numbers to alter the publishing rights. The only thing you know about this thief is that he is a well-known musician who can play the piano. Can you figure out who this musically talented thief is?
