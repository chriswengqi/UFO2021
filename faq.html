Q: I feel that the puzzles are boring and not engaging. How? :(

A: If you feel that you are not having fun with puzzles, can always contact your OGL! Maybe you and your OG can have other activities instead. Remember, the objective of the camp is for you to have fun! Life is not just about studying and competing with each other to win in everything :))

Q: Any tips to carry out the puzzle hunt in a team setting?

A: Good question! We highly encourage everybody in each team to participate in solving puzzles! Do not underestimate the power of teamwork! What we suggest is to:

  • Share screen and using the zoom annotation function.
  • Use notepad to visualise your thoughts.
  • Use google docs to note down pointers/record findings.
  • Use any other creative way if your team comes up with one!

When you are stuck, have you tried...

  • Alphabetizing
  • Caesar cipher-ing
  • Shifting letters with numbers
  • Reading the flavour text (extract) to source for clues.
  • Taking the 1st index of the 1st word, 2nd index of the 2nd word... or something similar to form a word?
  • Paying attention to the numbers in the pictures. Everything there is for a reason :) just like why me and you are in NUS :)
