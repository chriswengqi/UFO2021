Your Last Bow
You are back in the present. With all the experience and problem-solving skills that you have gained throughout your time-travelling journeys. You now believe that you have the skills to find who Derek’s murderer is.

But before that, you are to visit the final boss, Brexton who won all of Chris’ money (as shown in the promotional video). Brexton brings you to the poker table and shows you a set of turns, but apparently these turns are not related to poker at all. “Guess 1 out of 5 cards. ” he whispers to you, and leaves you to ponder upon the meaning of his words.

You realise that Brexton might be hinting you towards something, a trick that is quite elegant. Are you able to figure out what the murderer likes to do, and thereby deducing who the murderer is?
