The Unknown Solution from SOC
Many NUS researches are successful and widely known. You have recently heard that there exists an unknown solution in SOC for a few decades and scientists are still unable to figure out what it is. So you decided to travel back time and look into it. You asked your friend Ah Lian (who is a chemist) to help you check out what it is. However, you and Ah Lian got into a fight just before the result came out and she decided to unfriend you. She does not go easy with you and send you a weird message before letting you view the result. Can you figure out what the message is?
